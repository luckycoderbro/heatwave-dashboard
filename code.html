Heatwave Risk Factors & Analysis

Understanding the key environmental and urban parameters that drive heatwave vulnerability.

🌡️ Temperature

Measures the ambient air temperature in degrees Celsius

Impact: Higher temperatures directly increase heatwave intensity and stress on the human body

💧 Humidity

Represents the amount of moisture in the air

Impact: High humidity reduces the body’s ability to cool itself, increasing perceived heat (heat index)

🌫️ AQI (Air Quality Index)

Indicates the level of air pollution

Impact: Poor air quality worsens respiratory stress and increases vulnerability during heatwaves

🏙️ Density

Number of people per square kilometer

Impact: Higher density increases exposure and amplifies urban heat island effects

How It Works

A specialized sequence of data processing and machine learning to deliver accurate thermal intelligence.

1

Live data fetching

Ingesting global meteorological data from localized sensor networks and satellite arrays in real-time.

2

Heat index calculation

Combining temperature and humidity to determine the apparent 'feels-like' intensity across urban grids.

3

Risk score computation

Cross-referencing indices with historical norms and vulnerability metrics to generate a numerical score.

4

ML model classification

Our Random Forest models classify the risk into actionable severity tiers for immediate response.
